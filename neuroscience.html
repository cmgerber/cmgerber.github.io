Lab Work

The research was looking at relationships between dopamine and basal ganglia-thalamocortical system function and dysfunction, especially with respect to movement disorders such as Parkinson’s. Created programs in Spike 2 and Matlab to improve the efficiency of the data analysis process.

Worked on several different experiments doing surgeries, electrophysiological recordings, data analysis, and creating scripts to improve the efficiency of the process.
